About Us

Delivering Quality Healthcare To The Last Mile

We are all about collaboration of various health services to achieve a healthy and satisfied client in the shortest time possible at the clients convenience. Through trained caregivers,nurses and therapists, we offer wide range of mobile health services that enable our client receive the best quality care, we also consider those in remote areas who cant easily access the hospital care by taking health care services to them right at the there doorstep, for those who need further intervention we facilitate there transport to health care centers for the interventions. We also organize medical camps to create awareness on health related issues, prevention of diseases, and treatment to include de-worming, supplementation, weight monitoring, physiotherapy services to physically and mentally challenged children and prompt referral for clients who require hospital services with an aim to help improve quality of life

Vision

To be readily available and preferred mobile nurses and quality care providers to our clients anytime at their convenience

Mission

To prevent avoidable health complications like; cerebral palsy, Malnutrition, shock, Disability, infection or death through quick effective interventions and help them improve quality of life in health challenges they are faced with
